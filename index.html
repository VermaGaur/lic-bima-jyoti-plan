About LIC Bima Jyoti Plan

LIC Bima Jyoti Plan is a non-linked, non-participating, individual savings plan offered by the Life Insurance Corporation of India. It is a single premium plan that offers both life cover and guaranteed returns.

Features

  • Single premium payment
  • Life cover and guaranteed returns
  • No medical examination required for policyholders up to a certain age
  • Flexible policy term options
  • Loan facility available after 3 months of the policy
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961

Benefits

  • Life cover with guaranteed returns
  • Maturity benefit payable at the end of the policy term
  • Death benefit payable in case of the unfortunate demise of the policyholder
  • Tax benefits under Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act, 1961
  • Loan facility available after 3 months of the policy

FAQ

What is the minimum age to buy LIC Bima Jyoti Plan?

The minimum age to buy LIC Bima Jyoti Plan is 90 days.

What is the maximum age to buy LIC Bima Jyoti Plan?

The maximum age to buy LIC Bima Jyoti Plan is 60 years.

What is the policy term for LIC Bima Jyoti Plan?

The policy term for LIC Bima Jyoti Plan is 10 years or 15 years, depending on the policyholder's choice.

What is the premium payment term for LIC Bima Jyoti Plan?

The premium payment term for LIC Bima Jyoti Plan is single premium payment.
